Quick Action Strategy

Each page shows 2 carefully chosen actions:

  • Action 1: "What should I..." (insight/analysis)
  • Action 2: "How do I..." OR specific to the page

Examples by page:

  • Dashboard: "What should I focus on?" + "Analyze my progress"
  • Roadmap: "Show my progress" + "What should I study next?"
  • Lab: "Explain the concept" + "Help me set up"
  • Interview: "Practice common questions" + "Behavioral tips"

Expand Behavior (Cycling)

Click expand icon (⛶) to cycle:

  1. Click 1: Larger panel (600px width, more visible)
  2. Click 2: Fullscreen modal (full screen, same widget UI)
  3. Click 3: New tab (opens chat in separate browser tab)
  4. Click 4: Back to default floating size
